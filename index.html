    • Jae Van Aldover nicknamed "Jazz" (born Feb 15, 1998) is a very talented kid when it comes to coding. He's the third child with one sister and two brothers. Jazz currently lives in Sunvalley Paranaque City. His province is in Taal, Batangas where he go there now and then to visit his grand parents. After graduating from Malate Catholic School he enrolled in De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde getting a degree of BS- Information System. He enrolled BS-IS because he likes designing and programming in general, what a nerd (Kidding). His second choice was Multimedia because he also likes tweaking and he is good with editing. He is very good with Photoshop and video editing softwares, and I think that will come in handy in our work or any other work for that matter.

    Jazz's hobbies includes listening to music, playing computer games and travelling. In fact, Jazz started travelling when he was five years old and it was Hong Kong with his family. He likes travelling for the experience of the history of the place he goes to. Playing games is one the hobbies of many, Jazz's first game was Starcraft and he started playing when he was about 8 years old. He usually plays the popular game "Counter-Strike Global Offensive" and "Overwatch". His genre of games inludes First Person Shooter and MOBA. His genre of music includes Pop and classical music.

    Jazz usually sits at home listening to music or playing games because he does not want noisy environment. Jazz doesn't want to go to tight places because he is claustrophobic. Jazz is not very athletic and he rarely plays sports but he play tennis sometimes and he enjoys it mainly because its not a close contact game like basketball where you could get hurt. He is also into swimming.

    His current goal now is to graduate college and find a job that will suit his skill set like coding or editing. I ask what is his goal for the next 5-10 years and he answered that He want enjoy life and travel while he still has the energy and the capacity to do so. He wants to start a business someday in order for him to help his family and to fund his travelling expenditures in order for him to experience the world has to offer.
